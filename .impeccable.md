## Design Context

### Users
USAgentLeads serves B2B buyers who need real estate agent contact data: marketing agencies, SaaS and proptech teams, lenders, coaches, recruiters, and operators building outbound campaigns. They are usually comparing data quality, coverage, price, delivery speed, and trust signals before buying a CSV or subscription.

### Brand Personality
Practical, trustworthy, and direct. The product should feel like a reliable data utility: clear, fast to scan, commercially credible, and confident without sounding inflated.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light, refined SaaS interface with dense but calm information design. Use the existing blue accent, white surfaces, subtle borders, restrained shadows, Poppins for interface text, and JetBrains Mono only for compact data labels, counts, and codes. Avoid decorative gradients, oversized marketing drama, dark neon styling, and generic card-heavy fluff.

### Design Principles
- Make purchase-critical information instantly scannable: state, count, price, fields, and delivery.
- Prefer consistent operational UI over ornamental layouts; the audience is comparing data products, not browsing a lifestyle brand.
- Use restrained visual hierarchy: clean spacing, strong alignment, clear hover/focus states, and stable card dimensions.
- Treat trust as a design requirement: source notes, fields, pricing, and delivery should be visible and consistent.
- Keep mobile dense but readable, with touch-friendly controls and no horizontal overflow.
